Why are Tribal Schools in Trouble?

Native American young people have the lowest graduation rates in the US.

Native American tribal schools serve an important purpose. They are vehicles of cultural transmission, help meet the unique needs of Native youth, and reclaim education after years of suppression and brainwashing.
