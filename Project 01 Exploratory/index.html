PAINTINGS at MoMA

There are 2,078 paintings at the Museum of Modern Art. Each square here represents one painting, filled with the dominant color of the work and ordered by date.

The data comes from the MoMA Collection on GitHub, cleaned and prepared in Python. Since the dominant color was identified through machine learning, you may see errors -- it is imperfect compared to human perception!
